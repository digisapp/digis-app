CreatorCard Navigation Test

Open your browser console (F12) and click on a creator card on the Explore page.

  • [VanityRoute] Username: - Shows VanityRoute received the username

Common Issues:

  1. No logs at all - Click is being blocked by a button/form element
  2. Logs show but no navigation - profilePath is null or navigation is failing
  3. [VanityRoute] says "Invalid format" - Username has invalid characters or length
  4. [VanityRoute] says "Reserved word" - Username conflicts with a route

Quick Fix:

If you see "Invalid format", check the username in the database. It must be:

  • 3-30 characters
  • Only lowercase letters, numbers, dots, underscores, hyphens
  • Start and end with alphanumeric
